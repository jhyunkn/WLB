Wei / little school of better living

Not a tracker. A small school for becoming.

This board is for teaching, learning, inspiration, and better judgment. Open it when the phone is pulling you into noise. Leave with one clearer thought, one better question, or one small act of maturity.

The promise

No guilt dashboard. No endless checklist. This is a place that teaches you how to care for life: body, work, money, family, beauty, courage, and mood.

The method

Read one post-it. Let it provoke you. Answer one question. Then return to real life slightly more awake than before.

“The quality of your attention becomes the quality of your life.”

Daily Lesson

One teaching, one question, one way to live better today. This replaces scrolling with a small ritual of attention.

5 minute class
today's teaching
Mood is weather. Judgment is the driver.

You do not need to obey every feeling. Feelings report conditions; they should not automatically govern decisions. Pause long enough to separate what you feel from what you choose.

Ask: What would I do right now if I were calm, kind, and not trying to prove anything?

How to use today

  • Read the lesson slowly.
  • Answer the question honestly.
  • Do not optimize your whole life today.
  • Carry one sentence with you.

Wei's Life Curriculum

A small liberal-arts curriculum for better adulting: not to become impressive, but to become more awake, stable, capable, and interesting.

personal syllabus
module 01

Psychology of mood

Learn the difference between a feeling, a need, a fear, and a decision.

Practice: when your mood shifts, do not explain it with a dramatic story first. Ask: sleep? hunger? stress? uncertainty? social threat? Then decide.
module 02

GC judgment

Train yourself to see risk, sequence, ownership, and communication before problems harden.

The GC question: what is the issue, who owns it, what decision is needed, what happens if nobody moves, and what is the next visible action?
module 03

Financial adulthood

Money is protection, optionality, memory-making, and family stability.

A mature money life has layers: monthly clarity, emergency readiness, mortgage strategy, investing discipline, legal documents, and timely reimbursements.
module 04

Care of the body

The body is the instrument through which every ambition must pass.

Dentist, GI follow-up, ENT follow-up, pregnancy prep, contact lenses, sleep, and movement are infrastructure, not errands.
module 05

Memory design

Vacations and family time are how life becomes emotionally real.

Use PTO as a memory tool. Ask: who do I want to remember this year with?
module 06

Liberal arts life

To be cool like MIT is curiosity with discipline.

Read across psychology, philosophy, design, cities, behavior, and technology. The goal is range, not trivia.

Judgment Lab

Better life comes from better judgment: fewer mood-led decisions, clearer communication, stronger timing, and cleaner next actions.

think before reacting

The 4-question lens

  • What is actually happening?
  • What am I adding emotionally?
  • What decision is needed?
  • What is the smallest next action?

Before I speak

Can I say this in a way that is true, useful, and not just a discharge of my mood?

Good communication is not self-silencing. It is aim.

Feedback as education

  • What does good look like here?
  • Where am I unclear?
  • What should I improve next time?
  • Am I repeating the same mistake?

Care as Philosophy

Taking care of yourself is not a side task. It protects the person who has to live your life.

soft discipline
Care is not panic. Care is maintenance with love.

Appointments, routines, movement, rest, and pregnancy preparation are the quiet scaffolding under everything you want to become.

Question: what part of my body or life has been asking for attention, gently, for too long?

Health as infrastructure

  • Dentist is long-term maintenance.
  • GI problems deserve follow-up.
  • Possible acoustic neuroma deserves organized care.
  • Pregnancy prep deserves calm planning.
  • Movement is energy management.

Memory, Beauty, Atmosphere

The point is not merely to function. The point is to build a life with scenes worth remembering.

life as film

Wong Kar-wai lesson

Life is not only plot. It is atmosphere: light, timing, longing, music, food, walks, rooms, and the person beside you.

Prompt: what small scene would make this week feel more alive?

PTO as memory design

Do not treat vacation as escape only. Treat it as a way to make memories with Jun, your parents, and your brother while the year is still yours.

Small rituals

  • One dinner without phone.
  • One walk that becomes a conversation.
  • One family visit planned with care.
  • One photo printed, not just stored.

AI Mentor Prompts

Use AI as teacher, consultant, and reflection partner. Not to replace your judgment — to train it.

copy and ask

When overwhelmed

Help me separate facts, fears, responsibilities, and next actions. Be calm and direct. Reduce noise.

When learning

Teach me this like a liberal arts mini-class. Give me the idea, why it matters, a real-life example, and one reflection question.

When deciding

Help me think through this decision. Identify incentives, risks, emotional bias, timing, hidden costs, and the smallest smart next move.
