Cost decision checklist

Use this before booking either route.

1

Lock airports

Confirm whether both options use NYC ↔ SFO. If Option B changes airport, flights and car return may change materially.

2

Price lodging first

For July California travel, lodging near parks/coast often determines the real budget more than gas.

3

Check one-way fees

If Option B does not loop back to SFO, one-way rental fees can erase apparent savings.

4

Protect buffer

Keep at least $400–$800 buffer either way. Remote desert/mountain routes need more operational margin.
