Recommended sleep sequence

1 night SF, 1 night Yosemite area, 1 night Santa Cruz, 1 night Monterey/Carmel/Big Sur, 4 nights LA / Westside / LAX-area depending on budget and access.

Where to spend

Spend for Yosemite proximity and a good LA base. Save by avoiding Big Sur luxury lodging if prices are inflated; Monterey/Carmel can be more rational.

Where not to overdo it

Do not add San Diego unless Salk is essential. The LAX return makes San Diego a detour, not a natural endpoint.

Decision read

The new flights make the LA-ending version of Route C the strongest plan.

My recommendation

Commit to Route C as: San Francisco → Yosemite → Santa Cruz → Big Sur → Los Angeles. Treat Salk as optional. This gives you architecture, coast, and Yosemite without letting the route become overbuilt.

Critical next step

Price lodging by zone first: Yosemite area, Santa Cruz, Monterey/Carmel, then LA. If those numbers work, the route is structurally sound.
