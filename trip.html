Return car

Salk option

Salk is officially possible only with pre-registration and tour availability, but it is no longer clean with the LAX return.

If Salk is essential

Target Thursday 7/16 or Friday 7/17 for the 10:00 AM or 11:30 AM tour. That means LA → La Jolla → LA, which is a long day and should replace another LA activity, not stack on top of it.

If the route should stay clean

Skip Salk for this trip and keep LA as the final architectural base. Do Getty Wednesday, LACMA Thursday, and final LA buffer Friday.

Lodging strategy

Best staging for the LAX-return version.

Recommended sleep sequence

1 night SF, 1 night Yosemite area, 1 night Santa Cruz, 1 night Monterey/Carmel/Big Sur, 4 nights LA / Westside / LAX-area depending on budget and access.

Where to spend

Spend for Yosemite proximity and a good LA base. Save by avoiding Big Sur luxury lodging if prices are inflated; Monterey/Carmel can be more rational.

Where not to overdo it

Do not add San Diego unless Salk is essential. The LAX return makes San Diego a detour, not a natural endpoint.

Decision read

My recommendation

Commit to Route C as: San Francisco → Yosemite → Santa Cruz → Big Sur → Los Angeles. Use Getty on Wednesday, LACMA Thursday, and Friday as a flexible LA buffer. Treat Salk as optional.
