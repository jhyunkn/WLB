Decision read

The corrected flight assumption strengthens the Iceland case. Cost alone does not decide everything, but Iceland now has both a slight cost advantage and a cleaner route structure.

Choose California if
Epic Park Scale

You want the epic American park loop.

California is better if Yosemite, giant sequoias, and the desert-to-mountain arc matter more than efficiency. It is visually monumental but logistically heavier.

Choose Iceland if
Dense Scenic Route

You want density and less waste.

Iceland is better if you want a compact route with frequent major stops, cooler July weather, and less extreme transfer fatigue. With flights equalized at $1,000 total, it is also the cheaper option on paper.

My current lean
Value Pick

Iceland is the cleaner value choice.

With $500 per-person flights for both trips, Iceland becomes the stronger value-per-day option. California still wins for iconic national-park scale.
