Route C decision read

This is the most personally relevant design route: Yosemite plus California coast plus LACMA plus Salk. It is likely more expensive than Iceland and less nature-pure than the park loop, but it gives the strongest architecture/travel identity. Best if the trip is partly for inspiration, not just scenery.
